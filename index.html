BIO

Hi! My name is Andrew Schwan! I am a 15 year old programmer, writer and avid Ottawa Senators fan from the Ottawa Valley. I enjoy video game design, building computers, and hockey. I've participated in 4 game jams thus far and I am looking forward to participating in more of them in the future. I am also one of the founding members of Last Space Sandwich Studios.
